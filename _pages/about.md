## 🧑‍💻 About Me

Hi, I'm **Shaocheng Yan** — a **Ph.D. student** (2025–) at **Wuhan University**, advised by Prof. [Jiayuan Li](https://ljy-rs.github.io/web/).
My research moves from **3D reconstruction** toward **world models** — first, building **geometry-aware 3D representations** from raw sensors; next, modeling **how it evolves**.

Off the screen, you'll find me **chasing the wind on a bicycle** 🚴‍♂️, **hiking** 🥾 mountain trails, or just **walking with no destination** — somehow that's where my best ideas tend to show up.

Always open to collaboration — happy to chat research, side projects, or trail recommendations.

<div class="focus-tags"><strong>Focus:</strong> 3D Reconstruction · World Models · Robust Geometry · Visual Localization</div>
